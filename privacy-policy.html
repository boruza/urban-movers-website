Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Urban Movers and Cleaners! We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when booking our services or contacting us.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing addresses for service bookings.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: Data stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process service bookings and payments securely.
  • To communicate with you regarding your bookings or inquiries.
  • To improve our website and services by analyzing usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to protect sensitive data during transmission.
  • Regular security audits and updates to safeguard our website.
  • Access controls to limit data access to authorized personnel only.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Third-party service providers for payment processing and service management.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities, as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request updates or corrections to your data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at Urbanmoversandcleaners@gmail.com.
